My passions

I love nature, I love hiking and that's why my biggest passion is visiting the biggest and most famous mountains from Romania and soon from other countries.

My favorite mountain chain so far was situated in Rodna.

I've spent a few nights with a tent there and I absolutely loved the raw and beautiful sceneries it offered.

My plans are to visit more of this beautiful country and expand my horizons.

I've spent my entire childhood surounded by beautiful mountains and lakes and this is a big factor that contributed to my love for nature.

When I'm disconnected from everything I feel the most free and I take every second of this time re-connecting with nature.
